BD2K-LINCS DCIC at the 2016 BD2K All-Hands Meeting and Open Data Science Symposium

Members of the BD2K-LINCS Data Coordination and Integration Center participated in the BD2K All-Hands Meeting and Open Data Science Symposium in Bethesda, MD which took place on November 29 – December 1, 2016.

The BD2K All-Hands Meeting brought together researchers, educators, developers, and trainees funded by the BD2K Initiative. The goals of the All-Hands Meeting were to showcase the work being done by BD2K-sponsored programs and to build a cohesive BD2K consortium that maximizes synergies between participants.
