BD2K-LINCS 2018 Summer Research Training Program

All of the 2018 BD2K-LINCS DCIC Summer Fellows are conducting a faculty-mentored independent research project within a research laboratory affiliated with the BD2K-LINCS Data Coordination and Integration Center. Appointments were awarded on a competitive basis.

Presentation Session

This presentation session, hosted by the Ma'ayan Lab and the Mount Sinai Center for Bioinformatics, was held Thursday, August 9, 2018 at 10:00 AM Eastern Time at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York. Click here for the detailed agenda. This session featured presentations from the BD2K-LINCS 2018 Summer Research Fellows training in the Ma'ayan Lab.
